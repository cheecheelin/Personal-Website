Hey, nice to meet you!

I'm Cheechee - Product Designer, Globetrotter, and Avid Boba Drinker. I'm powered by milk tea and adventures.

Design, to me, is about solving problems. I'm absolutely invigorated by challenges that aim to tackle real world problems, and I believe in grounding design decisions in research.

I've lived in many places, including San Jose, Taipei, Los Angeles, London, and San Francisco. Having spent a decade in Asia and another decade in the US, I've had the opportunity to see the juxtaposition between East and West firsthand.

When I'm not at work, you can find me exploring new places + adventuring in the great outdoors. Most recently, I was in Barcelona, Tokyo, then Thailand.

I'm always down to collaborate on interesting projects or meet new people. Drop me a line here or hire me as a freelancer!
