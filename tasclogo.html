TASC- Logo Design


This is a logo I designed for the 2014-2015 TASC student group. The inspiration behind this logo design was the beautiful calligraphy of traditional characters, and I aimed to create this effect by modifying the outline of the Taiwan island.



Taiwanese Student Group Tshirt Design


This is a hand drawn and later vectorized design for a Taiwanese student group I was a part of my first year at UCLA. The entire design is in the shape of Taiwan and pulls in various elements native to the Taiwanese culture, such as the flag, boba, Taipei 101, famous landmarks, the night markets, and even the Taiwanese characters that symbolize "Taiwan". This design was worn by 20 staff members and also sold to the larger student body at UCLA.
